Flora and Fauna of Nicobar Islands

The Nicobar Islands are home to a rich diversity of flora and fauna, including unique species found nowhere else in the world. The islands' tropical climate and varied ecosystems support a wide range of plant and animal life.

Flora

  • Endemic species of trees such as the Nicobar Palaquium.
  • Diverse mangrove forests along the coastlines.
  • Rich undergrowth with various ferns and shrubs.

Fauna

  • Home to the Nicobar pigeon, a unique bird species
  • Presence of various reptiles, including sea turtles.
  • Diverse marine life in surrounding waters, including coral reefs.
